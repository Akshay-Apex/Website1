Formation and Power

Unravel the secrets behind these natural wonders: the forces that sculpt them, the types of waterfalls, and the impact they have on the environment.

  • Erosion by water: Over time, flowing water carves away rock, creating waterfalls.
  • Geological features: Cliffs, faults, and other formations can influence the shape and size of waterfalls.
  • Types of waterfalls: Plunging, cascading, horsetail, and many more, each with unique characteristics.
  • Environmental impact: Waterfalls create mist, regulate water flow, and provide habitat for diverse species.
